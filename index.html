One of my best projects is Android Non Root, which has three versions HTML, Markdown, and App. It is a tutorial that explores a range of powerful, open-source tools such as Termux, F-Droid, Shizuku, Tor, TrackerControl, InviZible Pro, and PipePipe to enhance your Android device’s functionality, security, privacy, and customization without the need for root access.

Secondly, my termux-sh project contains setup Shell scripts and related files for automating the configuration of Termux, a terminal emulator for Android. The scripts facilitate the installation of essential tools, environment configurations, proot distributions, QEMU virtual machines, and related setups. I am still working on it to make it more modular and customizable.

Also, I've made LaTeX-ToolKit, containing a LaTeX template for XeTeX and LuaTeX with custom commands, fonts, and CJK support, and other utilities.
